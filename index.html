After serving as a Duchesne County Commissioner for a decade as well as a Community Impact Board (CIB) member for five years. Ron is currently seving as State Senator for District 20. He brings unique knowledge and experience with infrastructural projects, including community/government structures, roads, bridges, and water/wastewater projects. He has been involved in landfill operations, travel and recreation plans, and other significant economic development efforts, as well as mitigation projects dealing with environmental risk. Ron has developed important relationships with decisionmakers throughout the state in multiple sectors, including state and national government agencies and tribal affairs. He is eager to provide direction and consultation in procuring funding and can lend support in networking tasks. Ron has held positions on both the Uintah Basin Association of Governments (UBAOG) and Tri-County Health Department boards. As such, he can assist in similar funding efforts through programs like the Community Development Block Grant (CBDG), Olene Walker Fund, soil conservation districts, and other state funds, along with federal funding opportunities such as Brownfield projects, NRCS, and USDA
